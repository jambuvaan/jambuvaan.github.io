How to start an Export business in India

in 6 easy steps

Jambuvaan / @jambuvaan

STEP 1 - Apply for PAN card

  • PAN (permanent account number) - unique number issued by Income tax department
  • Identification key for all financial transactions and indirectly prevents tax evasion by keeping a track of monetary transactions.

STEP 2 - Register your company

Register your company with Registrar of companies

STEP 3 - Get IEC Code

  • Register your company with Directorate General of Foreign trade
  • IEC Number is a unique 10 digit code required by the traders or manufacturers for the purpose of import and export in India
  • IEC number is mandatory for import export trade operations and also enable companies to acquire benefits from indian customs, export promotion councils council etc in India.

STEP 4 - Open a current account in your company name

A current account is meant for the for convenience of the business. They tend not to bear interest. Instead, a customer can deposit or withdraw any amount of money any number of times, subject to availability of funds.

STEP 5 - Register with Sales Tax authorities

Sales tax is exempted on exports. To claim the benefits of the sales tax exemption, it is essential to register with the Sales tax authorities.

STEP 6 - Perform market research

Targeting the right market on right time is highly essential for success.
